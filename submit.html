📏 How to measure trunk diameter:
1. Measure the circumference (distance around the trunk) using a tape measure or string
2. Divide by 3.14 (π) to get the diameter
Example: 94cm around ÷ 3.14 = 30cm diameter

Quick estimates:
🌱 Thin (can wrap one hand) = ~10–20cm
🌿 Medium (two hands to hug) = ~30–50cm
🌳 Large (can't reach around) = ~60–100cm
🌲 Massive (several people to hug) = 100cm+

📚 Quick Identification Tips
🌿Neem — Pinnate leaves, bitter smell, common everywhere
🍀Peepal — Heart-shaped leaf with long drip tip
🌸Gulmohar — Feathery leaves, red flowers in May
🌳Bargad — Aerial roots hanging from branches
🌻Amaltas — Bright yellow hanging flower clusters
🫐Jamun — Glossy oval leaves, dark purple berries
