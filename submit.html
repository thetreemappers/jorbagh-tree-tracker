  • Neem — Pinnate leaves, slightly bitter smell
  • Peepal — Heart-shaped leaf with long drip tip
  • Gulmohar — Feathery leaves, red-orange flowers (April-May)
  • Amaltas — Yellow hanging flower clusters right now!
  • Bargad — Aerial roots hanging from branches
