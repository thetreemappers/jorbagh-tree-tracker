How to Contribute

Anyone in Delhi can help build the most complete urban tree map the city has ever had.

01
📍
Find a Tree
Walk to any tree in your neighbourhood — on your street, in a park, outside your colony gate. If it's not on the map yet, it needs you.
02
🔬
Identify the Species
Not sure what tree it is? Use our AI identification tool — take a photo of the leaves or bark and it tells you the species instantly with confidence score.
03
🌱
Log It for Everyone
Tap GPS for precision coordinates, take a photo, fill in a few details. Your tree appears on the map immediately — visible to everyone in Delhi.
